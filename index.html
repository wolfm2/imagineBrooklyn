What is it about?

What can we achieve?

Central to the project, we are showcasing your stories, voices, and ideas on this website. It is our desire to develop a tool where neighbors can connect and share. Additionally, where possible, we intend to upstream our findings to local and state planning organizations to make your voice heard.
